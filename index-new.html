The Artist

Alex Crimson

With over 15 years mastering the dark art of tattooing, Alex Crimson creates haunting designs that merge gothic imagery with vibrant crimson accents. Each piece tells a story through the canvas of skin and the permanence of ink.

Specializing in macabre realism and neo-traditional horror, Alex's portfolio features skulls, timepieces, roses dripping with blood-red ink, and symbols from the darker side of symbolism and mythology.

Featured in international tattoo publications and a recipient of multiple convention awards, Alex's waiting list speaks to the demand for these distinctive pieces that blur the line between art and nightmare.
