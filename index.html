The Artist

Alex Crimson

With over 15 years of experience in the art of tattooing, Alex has mastered multiple styles including black & gray realism, neo-traditional, and Japanese irezumi.

Every piece is approached with passion and precision, creating custom designs that tell your unique story through ink and skin.

Specializing in dark imagery with vibrant red accents, Alex's work has been featured in international tattoo magazines and conventions across the country.
